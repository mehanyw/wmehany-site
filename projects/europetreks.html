EuropeTreks is an European touring company based out of Munich, Germany. In 2017, I went on one of their trips and at the end of it, I was in charge of creating a new website.
I was in charge of the design and development of this website.
I designed the website in Adobe Photoshop, created the intro videos in Adobe Premiere, and edited the videos in Adobe Lightroom. Then, I developed the website from scratch in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Programming Languages Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
Design Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Premiere.
