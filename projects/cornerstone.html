The Cornerstone PT

Client

The Cornerstone Physical Therapy

Date

October 2018

Category

Web

This clinic reached out to me to design and develop an easy to use website with telehealth functionality. In the website, you can schedule appointments, pay your bills online, and login to notify you of your appointments. The website also notifies the clinic via email when someone schedules an appointment.

The website was programmed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also included a few APIs to handle the calendar and payments functions.
Tools Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
